Choosing the best location:

The plot for construction of kvik shine car wash must have enough space to construct the facility and attractive.Following options should be noted while selecting a location:

  • Land area of about 300-400 sq.mts is required for construction.
  • Attractive place, near to busy streets and highways, shopping centers, supermarkets and so on.
  • The place chosen should be visible to everyone, so that customers can visit the washing facility.
  • The place should be free from traffic, so we can have easy access to maintain the car parking.
  • The place should locate near to large gatherings of people, like apartments, residential homes, townships with more number of population.

Agreement with land owner:

The land selected must have an agreement with the landlord, if the land has rented. After completion of signing the contract, we should take step forward for further permissions from government side. This form of contract is for longest period, usually for a term of 5 to 10 years.
