Choosing the attractive location:

The most common locations for placing restrobox are shopping malls, university campuses, movie theatres, railway stations, bus stations, hospitals, dorms, and other tiring venues that attract a lot of people.
It is also important that your chosen location is safe, well-lit and equipped with surveillance cameras. This way, you will protect your vending machines from theft and damage.

Agreement with place owner:

The place selected must have an agreement with the landlord, if the land has rented. After completion of signing the contract, we should take step forward for further permissions from government side.
