About Us

Restrobox is an inventive vending machine with smart access for vending requirements. Its basic function is to issue users with a wide range of products at all times. It delivers the product to customers based on selected product and amount paid for it.
Restrobox machines are fully automatic machines works based on different mechanisms for different types of machines. We have a first class crew of our Research and Development team. Our Research and Development team engineers are working hard to provide a high eminence of vending machines with a minimum price to our clients.

Some of our features are
  • Fully automatic, no manpower required.
  • Payment mode is completely online via., scanning the QR code generated.
  • UI on a touch screen
Our machines are marked for high performance and high efficiency.
