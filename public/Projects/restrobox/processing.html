Processing Machines

Processing machines are free standing vending machines used to prepare and provide Icecreams, tea and coffee in respective machines. It provides item based on selection of customer against the payment. Our machines are fully automatic, so do not require any manpower. Our different types of products are Snow cream ice-cream machine, Gola Slush ice-cream machine and Tea and Coffee Machine.

Snow Cream Icecream Machine

Snow Cream Icecream machine will provide soft Icecream based on the selected flavour and toppins. With our highly experienced and industry knowledge engineers, we are providing a high-quality range of Snow Cream Ice-cream Machine.
This machine mechanism works with compressor and air which makes ice-cream softy. Currently this machine has two chambers with 10 litres capacity each.
Icecream is dispensed in a cup, where a cup dispenser is provided at front end of machine. The price of icecream will be different for different flavours.
The touch screen is used in the product for easy and quick response. Clients can avail these products at lower price with high quality performance.

Location

The location for placing the processing products are mostly at public places such as shopping malls, movie theatres, railway stations, metro stations etc...

Gola Slush Icecream Machine

Gola Slush Icecream machine will provide slush Icecream based on the selected flavour and toppins. With our highly experienced and industry knowledge engineers, we are providing a high-quality range of Gola Slush ice-cream Machine.
This machine makes the slush with a mixture of Sugar and Water. The slush machine stirs or rotates the mixture at a constant speed so that the sugar and water molecules bond together before the water gets a chance to freeze. In this way, a soft, wet slurry mixture is formed.
Currently this machine has one chambers with 12 litres capacity for each flavour. Icecream is dispensed in a cup, where a cup dispenser is provided at front end of machine. The price of icecream will be different for different flavours.
The touch screen is used in the product for easy and quick response. Clients can avail these products at lower price with high quality performance.
