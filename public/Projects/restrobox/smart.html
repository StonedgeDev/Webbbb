Cigarette vending machine

Cigarette vending machine is a wall mountable machine which can be used for dispensing different types and brands of cigarette packs. This machine is useful for dispensing a whole pack of cigarette or a single cigarette with a machine holding capacity of more than 100 boxes. Stonedge is offering highly functional automatic cigarette vending machine that is highly appreciated for high performance and low maintenance. Our products has a smart design with its attractive look, transparent glass window and stainless steel body.
According to government rules, selling the cigarettes for only adults is allowed, so we are using a technology called Palm recognition and Face recognition to detect the age of customer.

Palm recognition

Palm recognition technique scans the palm veins through infrared camera and cross checks with the database and then dispenses item.

Age recognition

Age recognition technique uses algorithm to detect the age using facial landmarks and their eye positioning. Example, when a camera in mobile opens, it automatically shows age of a person.
