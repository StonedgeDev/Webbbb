Accessible Machines

Smart machines are free standing vending machines used to dispense the selected product available in the machine against the payment. Our products are packaged food items. Our machines are fully automatic operated and do not require any manpower. Our machines operation is easy to use, efficient performance and low maintainance. Our machines are Snacks and Drinks dispenser, Cool drinks dispenser, Bakery food dispenser, General goods dispenser, fruits and vegetables dispenser.

Snacks and Drinks Dispenser

Snacks and Drinks dispenser machine consists of snacks and drinks slotted in different trays for selection of product for an end user. These machines are used to deliver instant ready-to-eat snacks and soft drinks. Our product is constructed using high-quality components and materials.
Our machines are fully automatic and do not require any human assistance to operate. Our developed products are well known for their performance, user-friendliness, and consistency, as well as their low maintenance costs. We provide the product in a variety of quantities based on the client’s specifications and needs. Touch screen User Interface, which also provides instructions to customers to operate. Seperate slots for snacks and soft drinks. We are maintaing complete online payment mode.
